Rocks 6.1 beta is now available for evaluation. This preview is based upon CentOS Version 6.3 with all updates as 21 October, 2012 applied.

  1. To get started please download and burn the kernel iso.
  2. Boot your frontend with kernel iso that you just burned
  3. After you type "build" at the boot prompt, select beta6-x86-64.rocksclusters.org as your central server
  4. The following rolls are available. For your OS roll please select either os or [CentOS and Updates-CentOS-6.3]
