Preface

The JumpStart Roll installs and configures the frontend to be able to JumpStart™ a Solaris™-based back-end machine.

The JumpStart Roll supports 3 kinds of appliances.

  • Compute Appliance

  • NAS Appliance

  • Solaris Development Appliance
