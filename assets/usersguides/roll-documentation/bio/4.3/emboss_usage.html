3.5. EMBOSS

3.5.1. About

EMNOSS is the European Molecular Biology Open Software Suite, a set of tools that are used for sequence analysis by the Molecular Biology community (EMBnet).

3.5.2. Further Information

Information about using EMBOSS is available at http://emboss.sourceforge.net/. You may also register at their mailing list here.
