3.1. List of packages present in the Bio Roll

The Bio Roll contains a suite of Bio-informatics applications, most commonly in use by the bio-informatics community. The list of applications is as follows:

  • HMMER

  • NCBI BLAST

  • MpiBLAST

  • biopython

  • ClustalW

  • Mrbayes

  • T_Coffee

  • Emboss

  • Phylip

  • fasta

  • Glimmer

  • perl-bioperl

  • perl-bioperl-run

  • perl-bioperl-gui

  • perl-bioperl-db

All the packages that appear below are dependencies and are already present in the base and OS Rolls. They are installed automatically during system installation.
