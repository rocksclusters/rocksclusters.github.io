Roy Dragseth from the HPC Group at the University of Tromso has announced that the PBS Roll for Rocks v4.1 is released and is available for download.

Please see the roll homepage for useful tips and tricks.

Release notes:

  • Torque upgraded to 2.0.0p0
  • mpiexec upgraded to 0.80 + mvapich fix
  • insert-ethers --basename ...... is now supported so compute nodes can have any name.
  • Insertion and removal of nodes is now much smoother, mostly because of changes in torque

Many thanks to Steve Jones for beta testing and providing a build host for x86_64.
