There is now a dedicated mailing list for people building OptIPortals.
This list is being hosted by CalIT2 and can be found here.

The Rocks Viz Roll is currently a preferred method of building an OptIPortal. Users of the Viz Roll that have a strong interest in Tile Display Walls and middleware like SAGE may wish to subscribe to this list.
