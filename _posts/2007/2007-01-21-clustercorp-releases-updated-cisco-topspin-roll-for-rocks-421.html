Tim McIntire from Clustercorp announces:

Clustercorp has released an updated Cisco Topspin Roll for Rocks 4.2.1 for the x86_64 architecture:

  • Upgrades - Topspin Firmware Version 118 (previous version 110)
  • Fixes - Disables the OpenIB Daemon
  • Changes - The roll is separated into two versions; one for Rocks with RHEL and one for Rocks with CentOS.

See Clustercorp's Roll Downloads Page for ISO images and documentation.
