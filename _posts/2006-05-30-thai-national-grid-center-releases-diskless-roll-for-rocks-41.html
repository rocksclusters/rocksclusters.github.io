Somsak Sriprayoonsakul from Thai Grid announces:

Thai National Grid Center is pleased to announce the availability of Diskless Roll for NPACI Rocks 4.1. The Diskless Roll contains a new node type "compute-diskless" which makes ROCKS 4.1 support Diskless-type cluster. You can run MPI job or submit your SGE job to compute-diskless node as usual, the Roll is available on the Rocks downloads page, and information on the Roll can be found here.
