Roy Dragseth from the University of Tromso announces:

The PBS Roll has been updated.

Release notes

  • Support for insert-ethers flags --batch and --norestart is now implemented (useful when upgrading)
  • pbs_mom no longer continuously bangs on the server causing overload on the frontend during installation of new nodes
