Sun Microsystems and the Rocks Clusters group at UCSD are pleased to announce the release of the Sun Studio 12 update 1 Roll for Rocks 5.2.

The roll is available for download from the Sun Download Center.

The roll contains Sun Studio 12 update 1 compiler suite with C, C++ and Fortran compilers, Math libraries, and performance analysis tools with MPI support.

It is currently being made available for both Linux and Solaris Platforms.
