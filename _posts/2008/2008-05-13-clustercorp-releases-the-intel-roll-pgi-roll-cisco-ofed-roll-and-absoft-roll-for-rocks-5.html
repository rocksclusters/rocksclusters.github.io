Clustercorp Releases the Intel Roll, PGI Roll, Cisco-OFED Roll, and Absoft Roll for Rocks 5
Tim McIntire of Clustercorp announces:

Clustercorp has released the Intel Developer Roll (for Rocks 5.0, x86_64). The Intel Developer Roll includes Intel C, C++, Fortran Compilers, Runtime Components, and Developer Tools (v10.1).

Clustercorp has released the PGI Roll (for Rocks 5.0, x86_64). The PGI Roll includes Portland Group's C, C++, and Fortran Compilers (v7.1-6).

Clustercorp has released the Cisco-OFED Roll (for Rocks 5.0, x86_64). The Cisco-OFED Roll installs and configures OFED 1.3 InfiniBand stack and MPI layer as provided in the Cisco-OFED 1.3 RPMS and source code.

Clustercorp has released the Absoft Roll (for Rocks 5.0, x86_64). The Absoft Roll includes the Absoft Pro Fortran v10.1 Compiler Suite and associated libraries for x86_64 systems.

See the Clustercorp Roll Downloads Page to obtain the Rolls.
