June 2-3 2010
Rocks-A-Palooza V (Rocks All Hands Meeting)
Atkinson Hall (CalIT2), UCSD
La Jolla, CA

The fifth annual Rocks users meeting will be held on June 2nd and 3rd 2010 at UCSD in sunny La Jolla, California. The agenda, registration, and hotel details can be found here.

This is event is free for all participants from non-profit organizations, but you must register. Commercial participation is welcome, but as this meeting is supported by the NSF, attendees from for-profit organizations are required to pay a registration fee of $300 per person.
