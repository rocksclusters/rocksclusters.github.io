Raimondo Forlin from Quadrics announces:

Quadrics today announces the release of the Rocks 4.1 (Fuji) Roll.

In addition to the Quadrics device drivers and libraries, the new roll allows third party schedulers support and seamless integration with Sun Grid Engine.

Quadrics' Resource Management System (RMS), a licensed scheduler, is also included in the roll. RMS complements the unique ease of installation provided by Rocks, with simplicity of cluster administration.

Download

The roll is available for i386 and x86_64 architectures and can be downloaded at Quadrics web site.
