Roy Dragseth from the HPC Group at the University of Tromso announces:

The PBS Roll for Rocks v4.2.1 is released for i386 and x86_64 architectures.

Downloads

  • i386
  • x86_64

Release Notes

  • The roll contains the DNS fix from the Service Pack roll for 4.2.1. The PBS Roll can be installed standalone or along with the Service Pack roll, it won't matter.
  • Torque v2.1.2
  • Maui v3.2.6p16
  • pbstools v1.3 (only qpeek and qps integrated)
  • pbs_python v2.9.0
  • mpiexec 0.81

qpeek will list the output of a running job and qps will list the running processes of a job.
