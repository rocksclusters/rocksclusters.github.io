Episode 4 of RocksOn is Released.

RocksOn is a show produced by the developers of the Rocks Clusters software. Topics will range from current software development and what is running in the lab, to discussions on general computing.

Contents of this episode:

  • History Year 2.
  • Rocks 4.3 (Mars Hill) and Viz Roll is released.
  • Introduction of "Office Hours".
  • Development updates on Rocks on RHEL 5 and Solaris.
  • Announcement of SC BoF "Rocks Clusters BoF"
